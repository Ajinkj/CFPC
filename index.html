What is Carbon Footprint?

Carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gas emissions, mainly carbon dioxide (CO2), produced directly or indirectly by human activities. It is a measure of the impact individuals, organizations, or products have on the environment in terms of carbon emissions.

How to Reduce Carbon Footprint?

There are several ways to reduce your carbon footprint, including:

  • Reducing electricity consumption
  • Using public transportation or carpooling
  • Switching to energy-efficient appliances and vehicles
  • Adopting sustainable practices like recycling and composting
  • Eating less meat and choosing locally sourced, organic food
